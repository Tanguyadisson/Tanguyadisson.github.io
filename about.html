02 : About me

Who am I?

I am a 19 year old high level sportsman, who practices canoe slalom and kayak cross.

Path

I grew up with my family in the High Alps, in a context of sport and nature. Skiing, kayaking, cycling and hiking were the rhythm of my days. It is on the basin of L'argentière-la-bessée that I gave my first paddle strokes. I then joined the "Pôle Espoir" in Nancy, which allowed me to train more regularly, to meet other motivated athletes, and to win my first medals. Two years later, I joined the Vaires-sur-Marne university centre, where I was selected for the French national team. This selection allowed me to join the "Pôle France", the best French training centre, where I am still training today.

Daily

I am training today in Vaires-sur-Marne, on the water stadium that will host the Paris 2024 Olympic Games. With all the French canoe slalom dynamics turning towards this city, it is the ideal place to progress with the best athletes and the best French coaches.
